Steak!

  • USDA Top Choice Rib Eye Steak
  • Ground sea salt
  • Fresh ground black pepper
    1. Let steak warm to room temperature
    2. Pat dry with paper towels allow seasoning to better stick
    3. Rub with olive oil
    4. Rub with desired seasoning mix
    5. Heat on medium high heat for 3 minutes then flip
    6. Allow steak to rest away from the heat
    7. Slice and enjoy
